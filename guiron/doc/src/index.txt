Introduction
============

The Gurion toolkit is intended to add fourier transform functionality and
helping functions to Gamera without adding the dependancy of the FFTW 
library in the main Gamera distribution.

  `Guiron website`__.

.. __: http://dkc.jhu.edu/gamera
